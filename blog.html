Recalling my Summer Abroad. 11/05/2020

The summer preamble to my academic career at Oregon State University was a life changing experience. I had the amazing opportunity to travel to London to study abroad in one of the most storied cities of the western world. Going to this place fulfilled a childhood dream of mine. I grew up fascinated by British history, specifically the events of the Second World War. I can recall many a night sat in front of the TV watching any and every BBC program that OPB was playing with my family. I loved the accents, the atmosphere, and mostly just the idea of London.

This blog is a work in progress, as is this post. I will add more to this post when I get a chance. I'm hoping to figure out some way to automate my formatting so that I don't have to type these into Visual Studio.
